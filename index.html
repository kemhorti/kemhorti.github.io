History

Sri Krishnarajendra Hill Station (SKRHS) - started by His Highness Sri Krishnarajendra Wodeyar of Mysore in 1932, as a summer resort by building Sri Dattatreya Bhavan. His Highness gifted the hill station to then government in 1942.

About

The hill station is at an altitude of 1434 meters above MSL. Mythologically and historically this hill range is known as “CHANDRA DORNA PARVATHA” and famous as “QUEEN OF HILL STATIONS”. The hill station is surrounded by Bhadra Wild Life Sanctuary (Tiger Core Habitat), forest lands and private coffee estates.

Places to Visit

The important sight seeing places around Kemmannugundi are – Kallatti Falls and Sri Veerabhadreshwara Cave Temple, Hebbe Falls, Jagara Pass, and Jagara Valley, Datttatreya Peetha / Baba Budan Giri, Mullayana Giri, Manikyadhara, Honnammana Halla, Deveeramma Temple, Amruteshwara Temple.
